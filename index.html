About Me

I'm a software developer based in Los Angeles, CA!

Born in New York City, I was instilled early on with the belief that we should leave a space the same or better than we left it. I continue take this principle with me wherever I can, finding any big or small way to bring simplicity and happiness into the lives and communities I find myself in.

If you are here, that means we're officially friends, and I'd love to hear from you! Whether it's boosting the web presence of your personal brand or business endeavor, or working together as a full-time engineer, I want to see your dreams become a reality. Continue scrolling to see the type of work I do, or let's connect and gush about your ideas!

When we stop that rabbit hole for a second, let's chat about whether you listen to the melody or lyrics more when you find a new song, how cute this sesame imo inu is, or why it's so hard to find good pizza in LA. 🍕
